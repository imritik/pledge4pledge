Pffledge4Pledge


Pffledge4Pledge is an online healthcare networking and health services aggregator company aiming to connect and establish a professional healthcare ecosystem with a network of markets and business around them.


Vision

We ‘Pledge’ to bring all the healthcare fraternity on a single platform and make them accessible with the populations in all the sectors to deliver not just the health opinions and quality medical advice at affordable but also share their valuable researches, experiences and learnings through innovative and disruptive technologies.



A unique platform for medical professionals to share and discuss cases, latest researches and network among themselves as well as be able to share their publishings with non medical professional and respond to the job opportunities posted by various healthcare recruiters. The platform also enables the interested medical professionals to give an online medical opinion to the patients based on their schedule availability which is a paid service.
